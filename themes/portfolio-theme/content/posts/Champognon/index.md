Champognon is a game board prototype. It was made as a school project by a team of 4 people, including myself.

Carrousel general

It is a simple ressource managment turn based game where each player has to choose what to grow in order make combos of goods that can sell for astronomic amounts of money.

Image menu and crops

You can only grow goods during their respective season, and planning ahead is the key to sucess. If you feel that a player have the upper hand, sabotage can be an option to slow them down
